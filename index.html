About Keeelo
Weekly running schedule

Keeelo [กี่โล] In Thai means 'How many Kilometer?' Made by a designer who runs as hobby, for hobby runners. Keeelo helps you put together a weekly running plan using a structured microcycle approach — but it wasn't built by a coach and isn't meant for serious or competitive training. So treat it as a personal guide rather than expert advice.
